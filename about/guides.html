Guides

Miss Christina

Lead Guide

Miss Christina started as an assistant guide at AMS in 2010. She has been the lead guide since 2018. Prior to AMS, Miss Christina taught kindergarten in the public school system.

Miss Christina holds a bachelor's degree in elementary education from Big State U and is certified as a lead guide through The Montessori Training Center of Minnesota.

Miss Emily

Assistant Guide

Miss Emily has been an integral member of the AMS community since 2019. Prior to discovering her passion for Montessori education, Miss Emily worked for two years as a graphic designer.

Miss Emily holds a bachelor's degree in graphic design from Liberal Arts College. She completed her assistant guide certification in 2019 at the Montessori Institute of Milwaukee.

Miss Sophia

Assistant Guide

Miss Sophia became interested in Montessori educational methods when her two daughters were toddlers. She has been an assistant guide at AMS since 2021.

Miss Sophia holds a bachelor's degree from Small Music Conservatory in piano performance. Her assistant guide certification is through Midwest Montessori Teacher Training Center.
