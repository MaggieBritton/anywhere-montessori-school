Tuition & Financial Aid

Anywhere Montessori School is a non-profit school. We promise to keep tuition rates as low as possible while still providing your child(ren) with a high-quality Montessori education.

Enrollment Deposit

A $100 non-refundable registration fee per family is due upon completion of the enrollment application. The registration fee is credited towards the first tuition payment in September.

Tuition Discounts

Families with two children enrolled at AMS will receive a 5% discount on the second child's tuition. A third child's tuition will be discounted at 10%. The discount will always apply to the child(ren) with the lowest tuition.

Financial Aid

It is a goal of Anywhere Montessori School to provide quality education to students of all financial backgrounds. The AMS School Board has therefore allocated funds to be used for tuition scholarships based on financial need.
